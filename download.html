Download fAIrewall

fAIrewall requires two components: a desktop app (the local backend) and a browser extension.

Step 1 — Desktop App

The desktop app runs locally on your machine. It performs all PII detection, sanitization, and audit logging. When you use a supported AI provider, only the minimized prompt is sent directly to that provider — never to fAIrewall servers.

Step 2 — Browser Extension

The browser extension intercepts prompts before they reach the AI provider and sends them to the local desktop app for sanitization.
