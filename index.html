AI adoption is moving faster than data control.

AI tools are already part of daily work. The challenge is not just model quality. It is what gets exposed along the way. Personal data, internal identifiers, customer information, and sensitive business context often reach AI tools before governance does.

fAIrewall is built for that reality. It adds a local layer of control before prompts are sent, helping reduce unnecessary exposure without asking users to abandon the tools they already rely on.
