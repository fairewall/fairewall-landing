Privacy Policy

This website is operated by fAIrewall. We aim to keep data collection minimal and aligned with the product's local-first privacy model.

Last updated: April 2026

What this policy covers

This policy explains how we handle personal data collected through this website, direct email contact, and Early Access requests. It does not override the local-first design goals of the product itself.

Information you may provide

If you contact us, request Early Access, or email support, we may receive the information you choose to provide, such as your name, email address, company name, and message content.

What we do not collect by default

We do not use unnecessary analytics, advertising trackers, or behavioral profiling on this website.

Product data

fAIrewall is designed around local processing and local control. We do not position the product as a cloud service for handling your prompts by default. If you voluntarily send support materials, logs, screenshots, or related information, we will use them only for support, troubleshooting, or security review.

Why we process personal data

We may process personal data to respond to inquiries, manage Early Access requests, provide support, improve communications, maintain security, and meet legal obligations where required.

Legal bases

Where applicable under GDPR, we rely on consent, legitimate interests, contractual necessity, or legal obligations, depending on the context of the interaction.

Data retention

We keep personal data only for as long as reasonably necessary to handle the request, maintain relevant records, comply with legal obligations, or support legitimate business and security needs.

Third-party services

Our hosting and email providers may process limited technical or communication data as part of website delivery and message handling. We aim to keep third-party exposure minimal.

Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, restrict, or object to certain processing of your personal data. You may also contact us about privacy-related requests.
